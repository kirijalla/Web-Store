About Vynil Store

We are passionate about music and the warm sound of vinyl records. Since 2020, we've been bringing the best records and audio equipment to music lovers in Montreal and beyond.

Our Mission

To make high-quality vinyl records and audio equipment accessible to every music lover, and to keep the culture of analog sound alive.

Our Vision

To become the go-to destination for vinyl enthusiasts across Canada, building a community around the love of music.
